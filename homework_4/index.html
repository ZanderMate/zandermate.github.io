The Rules of the Alex Quiz:

  1. Each question is multiple choice with 4 possible answers.
  2. If you get the question right, you move onto the next question and gain 1 points.
  3. If you get the question wrong, you move onto the next question, gaining no points.
  4. Once you press the 'Begin' button, the quiz will begin.

The Rules of the Movie Quiz:

  1. Each question is multiple choice with 4 possible answers.
  2. If you get the question right, you move onto the next question and gain points base on how fast you answer the question.
  3. If you get the question wrong, you lose ten seconds from the timer and move onto the next question, gaining no points.
  4. Once you press the 'Begin' button, your timer will start at fifty seconds.
