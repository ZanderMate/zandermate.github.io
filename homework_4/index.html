Movie Trivia Time!

The Rules of the Quiz:

  1. Each question is multiple choice with 4 possible answers.
  2. If you get the question right, you move onto the next question and gain 2 points.
  3. If you get the question wrong, you lose ten seconds from the timer and move onto the next question, gaining no points.
  4. Once you press the 'Begin' button, your timer will start at sixty seconds.
